SPACECRAFT

A spacecraft is a vehicle or machine designed to fly in outer space. A type of artificial satellite, spacecraft are used for a variety of purposes.

INVENTION

No one person invented the first spacecraft, but rather it was the work of a very large team. The first ship to safely put a man in space and orbit the earth was Vostok 1, piloted by Yuri Gagarin in 1961

WORKING

The launch of a spacecraft comprises a period of powered flight during which the vehicle rises above Earth's atmosphere and accelerates at least to orbital velocity.
